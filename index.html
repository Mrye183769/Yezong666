选择的幻觉：日常设计中的权力

The illusion of choice: power in everyday design
                                                                                                   

——《幻视 . Visual hallucination》——

学生作品介绍Introduction of student works   —— 3190601172  叶以兴

背景-低头族
The low-head people

在如今的社会，智能的手机的便利给人类带来很多的好处。但是越来越多的人成为低头族——（经常低着头看手机），即大部分人走在路上也离不开手机，长时间低头看手机走路不仅仅会造成近视，而且会造成颈椎疾病和交通事故等严重的后果.
In today's society, the convenience of smart phones brings many benefits to mankind. However, more and more people are becoming low-head people-(they often look at their mobile phones with their heads down). That is most people cannot walk on the road without their mobile phones. Looking down at their mobile phones for a long time will not only cause a near-sighted eyes, but also will cause a serious consequences such as cervical vertebra diseases and traffic accidents.  

研究目标 Research objectives.

通过分析“低头族”现象，尝试设计出对应的新概念产品，以缓解这种现象。
By analyzing the phenomenon of "low-headed people", I try to design corresponding new concept products to alleviate this phenomenon.

—— 研究方向 Research direction ——

设计师如何利用消费者的选择创造最佳体验？ 作为设计师，你如何创造性地体验这种张力？

How can designers leverage the uses of consumers' choices to create optimal experience?
How can you as a designer experiment with this tension creatively?

设计构想Design conception

——产品概念Product concept —— 

 

智能手机轻巧便利的设计看似提供了多样的使用体验，实际上人们只能通过低头看的方式来使用，这形成了一种选择上的错觉，并且对于长时间使用者非常不利。所以我想要制作一个概念产品，这个产品的核心在于它解放了人们的双手，不需要低着头去看，当它检测到你走路时低头使用产品，就会通过强烈的震动反馈阻止你低头，缓解“低头族”现象。并且这个产品会提供给用户更多不同的更加个性化选择，以创造最佳的用户体验。

   

Lightweight and convenient design seems to provide a variety of use experiences, in fact, people can only use it by looking down, which forms a false choice, and is very unfavorable to users for a long time. Therefore, I want to make a conceptual product. The core of this product is that it frees people's hands and doesn't need to look down. When it detects that you are using the product while walking, it will stop you from bowing down through strong vibration feedback and alleviate the phenomenon of "low-headed people". And this product will provide users with more different and more personalized choices to create the best user experience.

——产品定位Product positioning ——

功能定位
Functional orientation

这是一个移动智能设备，就像手机一样，可以满足日常生活需求的同时，崭新的拓展记住与功能会带来更加惊艳的体验。
This is a mobile intelligent device, just like a mobile phone, which can meet the needs of daily life. At the same time, the brand-new expanded memory and function will bring more amazing experience.

用户群体定位
User group positioning

使用群体主要是上班族，技术研究人员，和学生群体，他们比较青睐这类新型数字产品。
The users are mainly office workers, technical researchers and students, who prefer this new type of digital products

使用场景定位
Functional orientation

使用环境限制较小，主要在室内使用观感更佳，室外使用会有“杜绝低头族的限制”，暂时不支持在飞机上或者水中使用。
The use environment is less limited, and it has a better look and feel mainly in indoor use. Outdoor use will have the restriction of "putting an end to low-headed people", and it is temporarily not supported to be used on airplanes or in water.

——主要功能介绍Product vision——

Head down detection and vibration feedback.

设备佩戴在头部，后面的脊椎检测器会配合前面的摄像头检测使用者是否在路上边低头边使用设备，并给出震动反馈。 The equipment is worn on the head, and the back spine detector will cooperate with the front camera to detect whether the user is using the equipment while lowering his head on the road, and give vibration feedback. 

Front-projected holographic display.

信息以投影的方式显示，设备内置触觉感应器，可用手指直接对投影进行操作,解放双手，给予用户更多的选择。 Information is displayed in the form of projection, and the device has built-in tactile sensor, which can be directly operated by fingers, giving users more choices. 

Desktop mode and free mode.

产品有两种模式，桌面模式和自由模式，可满足不同的使用需求。只需将设备投影到桌面上后即可且换桌面模式。 The product has two modes, desktop mode and free mode, which can meet different use requirements. Just project the device onto the desktop and change the desktop mode. 

设计过程Process

 

低头族The low-head people

分析现象成因，寻找与课题的内在联系，确定研究方向和目标。
Analyze the causes of the phenomenon, find the internal relationship with the subject, and determine the research direction and goal

提出产品概念product concept

提出并逐步完善产品概念，设计产品原型，绘制草图和初步效果图
Put forward and gradually improve the product concept, design product prototype, draw sketches and preliminary renderings

用户调研User research

通过用户调研与实验明确产品定位，功能缺陷和一些细节问题
Identify product positioning, functional defects and some details through user research and experiments

整理导出Organize and export

最后整理产品框架，完善逻辑理论，绘制设计图纸和制作说明书成品。
Finally, organize the product framework, perfect the logic theory, draw the design drawings and make the finished product of the manual.
